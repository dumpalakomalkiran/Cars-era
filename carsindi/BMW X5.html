BMW X5 Review

Summary

BMW is amongst the three best-selling luxury automakers in the world and has been producing luxury automobiles for approximately 100 years. BMW X5 is yet another example of highly efficient production mechanism that has been customized according to the luxurious needs of people. BMW X5 has been tailor made to fit a distinctive combination of price and luxury alongwith power and elegance.BMW X5 is a unique combination of ergonomics, elegant ambience and best quality exterior and interior designs. The efficiency of the engine is kept high, so as users can scale any road with utmost ease and speed. BMW X5 can also be configured by one as per his/her needs and wish. This personalized configuration facility furthermore enables one to dictate the roads as per capability and desire. Moreover, in this way customers get their choice cars alongwith thebest quality engineered BMW automobiles.

Further to this, read BMW X5 Road Test details by our expert.

Exterior

BMW X5 is dynamically designed with upright proportions. The design is dynamic and unbeatably versatile although from the front, leaping forth from sides to the rear. X5 offers a majestic appearance that has its elegance and snob appeal. BMW X5 has been designed keeping one important factor in consideration, and that is the aerodynamics; which not only helps it attaininghighspeeds but also offers stability at these speeds. X5 has an eye-catching and attractive, ideally optimized design with certain other elements in the front end, the sides and the rear. Large air inlets, the recently developed Air Breather in the front wheel arches and the Air Curtain in the front Apron not only underlines the all new sport outlook of this vehicle but also helps in cutting down fuel consumption and harmful emissions.

X5 offers an ideal aerodynamic shape in therear also, for which it has air Blade on the D-pillar. This feature adds to it look wider and also improves airflow, rendering stable aerodynamics in theoverall structure of this vehicle. For athletic appearance, the wide design of the BMW kidney grille at the front offers an additional imposing. The complementary nature of the headlights and the kidney grille gives an impression of a unique all-in-one unit that emphasizes the vehicle’s wide track. Few exterior types of equipment include headlight washer system, rain sensor and automatic driving lights, adaptive LED headlights and exterior mirrors.

Interior

Along-with sporty and athletic exterior one can experience even more classy interiors as soon as one opens the door of this BMW X5. For maximum comfort, there are top-quality, leather refined structures which are complemented by atmospheric lighting for a distinctive exclusiveness.

While the exteriors of the X5 were marked by their imposing bodywork, the interiors of this masterpiece are dominated by pounding elegance that certainly has its snob appeal. The interiors are marked with all modernistic facilities and comfortable design. The widened space feelings are elevated through horizontal contours and contrasting surfaces. All materials used in BMW X5 is of top-notch quality, anda huge collection of upholstery combined with atmospheric lighting, induces a sense of unrivalled feel-good factor.

The seats and space are complementarily designed so that riders get a pleasant experience in every ride. Seats are so adjusted that one can see theroad ahead. Additionally, other technological advancements such as Navigation System, iDrive Touch Controller and professional Control Display are oriented towards the driver seat for utmost ease. For more comfort, mirrors come with anti-glare function. Other interior equipment in the BMW X5 includes armrest comfort package, smokers package, interior mirrors with anti-dazzle function, gearshift paddles, rear seat storage compartment and leather steering wheel. There is afacility for BMW apps and audio operation at therear side also.

Engine and Transmission

BMW X5 gains its power from an even more powerful engine that comes with four cylinders and four valves. The maximum total output is 230 kW or 313 hp. Maximum attainable torque is 450 Nm, and the electric motor’s maximum output is 83/3170 in kW at 1/min. The fuel tank capacity is approximately 85 litres. For smooth transmission even more powerful tools and technologies have been deployed.

Performance and Handling

Due to BMW X5 powerful engine and shift transmission, the performance is highly scalable with a top speed of 210 km/h having an acceleration time from 0-100 km/h speed as low as 6.8 seconds. Handling becomes even easier with a powerful braking system combined with best-in-class aerodynamic design. Fuel consumption combined is 3.4-3.3 in L/100 km. The eDrive energy consumption is 15.4-15.3 kWh/100 km.

Braking System

The braking system in BMW X5 is very robust and capable of bring this machine to halt from top speed in seconds with one touch of the brake pedal. It happens with aperfect precision that takes place via the Black enamelled aluminium brake callipers. There are fixed calliper 395 mm brakes at the front axle complemented by floating calliper 385 mm brakes in the rear end at the rear axle. This braking system features ensure that customers get comfortable driving experience each time.

Mileage

The 2993 cc Diesel - powered variant delivers a fuel economy of 14 kmpl in city and 16 kmpl on highways, while the Petrol variant offers a mileage of 15 kmpl on urban roads and 17 kmpl and freeways, respectively.

Safety Features

For a machine such as a luxurious automobile car that too with athletic outlook, effective security and safety measures are needed tobe incorporated. Thus, BMW X5 supports various safety measures to make the machine even more reliable. Safety measures include the anti-lock braking system, central locking, parking sensors both in front and the rear, driver airbag alongwith side airbag in front. There are also side impact beams and the front impact beams. Automatic headlamps, traction control, crash sensor and door ajar warning facility is also present.

Verdict

BMW X5 is a niche choice if one is looking for elegant style car that comes with all modernistic facilities. Thus, it is the best buy given the price range you want to invest.

Competition

BMW X5 sees competition with Audi Q7, Mercedes-Benz M-class and Volvo XC 90. But still it enjoys as the most rated car among them considering cost and features.
